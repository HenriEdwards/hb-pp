Summary

The App is designed to work offline. We do not require accounts, and we do not collect or sell personal data.

Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information from you through the App.

Data you enter in the App

Any habits, notes, reminders, or settings you create are stored locally on your device (for example in on-device storage/database). We do not receive this data.

How your data is used

  • To provide the App’s features on your device (tracking, stats, calendar, reminders, etc.).
  • To save your preferences locally (language, theme, settings).

Sharing of information

We do not share your personal information because we do not collect it.

Third-party services

The App does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs to track you.

If the App includes optional services in the future (for example crash reporting, analytics, cloud sync), this policy will be updated before those changes take effect.

In-app purchases

If you purchase a premium upgrade, the transaction is processed by the app store (e.g., Google Play). We do not receive your full payment details. Store providers may collect information as described in their own policies.

Permissions

The App may request device permissions only to enable features you choose to use (for example, notifications for reminders). Permissions are handled by your device operating system, and you can enable/disable them in your device settings.

Data retention

Your App data remains on your device until you delete it, clear the App’s storage, or uninstall the App.

Security

We take reasonable measures in the App’s design to avoid collecting data and to keep your information on-device. No method of storage is 100% secure, but because your data stays on your device, you remain in control.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International users

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not transfer your personal information to our servers.

Changes to this policy

If we change this Privacy Policy, we will update the “Last updated” date and post the new policy on this page.
